The AVE-RD video series, in both the **Informative** and **Persuasive** variants, was distributed to participating schools through the Department of Orientation and Psychology (DOP) of the Ministry of Education.

If you are a counselor, teacher or DOP technician and you need access to the video material:

1. Confirm with your DOP regional coordinator that your school is part of the AVE-RD pilot.
2. Request the deployment package from the project team using the contact details on the [Contact](/projects/contact/) page.
3. The team will provide a download link and the implementation guide for the relevant grade levels (7th–12th).

If you are a researcher or partner organization interested in using the AVE-RD materials for evaluation or replication, please reach out via e-mail and describe your intended use.
